Why Get a Free Energy Audit?

You can become a crucial part of the energy transition! Our comprehensive free audit program is designed to provide property owners with valuable energy insights and potentially free/discounted upgrades, while also serving as a hands-on learning opportunity for our students. By allowing us to use your property for training future energy auditors, you are not only enhancing your own energy efficiency, but you are contributing to the education and future careers for our green collar professionals.

  • Lower Energy Bills
  • Increase Property Value
  • Free or Discounted Upgrades
  • Environmental Impact

How does it work?

1. Initial Consult and Property Assessment

Schedule an initial consultation to discuss your property needs and goals. Our team conducts a preliminary assessment to determine suitability for the program.

2. Comprehensive Energy Audit

A team of trained students, supervised by industry experts, performs a thorough energy audit of your property. You will receive a detailed report outlining energy usage patterns and recommendations for improvements.

3. Implement Upgrades

We identify opportunities for upgrades and provide free or discounted clean energy options. Students, guided by professionals, can implement recommended upgrades while ensuring high-quality work and effective solutions.
